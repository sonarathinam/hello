ABOUT US

PVR Cimemas is an entertainment ticketing platform developed by Bigtree Entertainment. Its platform allows users to buy tickets for cinemas, theaters, and sports. The company aims to deliver entertainment experience to customers.Launched in 1999, PVR, owned and operated by Big Tree Entertainment Pvt Ltd, is India's largest online entertainment ticketing platform that allows users to book tickets for movies, plays, sports, and live events through its website, mobile app and mobile site. PVR is headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.
